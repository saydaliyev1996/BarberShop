OUR STORY

About us

BarberShop classic barbershop was founded in 2019 in Tashkent. Tashkent Barbershop is a classic upscale barbershop. We offer a variety of services, catering to everyone. We are barbers who love our craft, providing top notch service that helps men Stay Sharp.

Our Service

HAIRSTYLING

We have an uncanny ability to listen. Being a competent hairdresser entails paying attention to our customers in a variety of ways. And bringing the hairstyle of your dreams to life.

SCALP MASSAGE

Our scalp massage increases blood flow and oxygen to the hair follicle, allowing more nutrients to reach the root and promoting hair growth. Our special Scalp massages are very effective for people who have thinning hair or hair loss.

EDGE UP

Our shape-up service, also known as a line-up or an edge-up, entails straightening the natural hairline by cutting along it. The basic outline for haircuts today is shape-ups or edge-ups.

HAIR CUT

There are numerous aspects that influence how we do unique hairdos. Face shape, hair type, growth patterns, cowlicks, and, most importantly, lifestyle are all significant factors to consider.

BEARD TRIM

We enjoy beard grooming because that helps you prevent frizzy hair, split ends, roughness, and shabbiness. It ensures that your beard matches your face on a daily basis, especially if it is in good shape.

COLOURING

A hair colorant that is safe for your hair is used. It shouldn't injure the hair shaft, but it should color it without changing its natural structure or shine.
